Logan Circle

Little Gay Pub 5/5
The Le Dip of gay bars. Lots of cool history, very well defined vibe, the occasional celeb (I met the Irish PM here), take a bathroom selfie! Very much a cocktail bar.
Number 9 4/5
Dangerously strong mixed drinks, upstairs is young and downstairs runs older, people don't really dance here, just stand and talk
Trade 3.5/5
The closest thing DC had to a leather bar, cute back patio, cheap drinks, some dancing, patio is all chit chat
Green Lantern 3.5/5
Dive bar in an alley, known for shirtless Thursdays (free drinks from 10-11 if you're shirtless, and free drinks from 12:00-12:30 if you're in your undies), cheap drinks, upstairs is the dance floor
Crew Club
DC's bathhouse, I've never been but I've heard it's grimy.

14th and U

Bunker 4/5
DC's premier (and only) gay nightclub, very circuity, def a place to go if you like to dance. In my opinion, their sound system is not equalized properly and is ridiculously bass heavy (but this could apply to a lot of gay bars)
Crush 4/5
DC's newest bar with great orange cocktails, fun pop music vibe (very expensive IMO), first floor is dancing and the rest is dance/talk
Thurst 4/5
DC's only Black-owned Black gay bar, good cocktails, I've only been once so I can't speak to it too much, but it's fun
Licht 5/5
Cute cafe/bar, very intimate vibe, lots of gay nudist art on the walls. Wonderful place to chat if you can find a seat.
The District Eagle 4/5
Just opened at the end of January 2025, has Dark Room. Great sound system but could be louder. It is much better when the upstairs is open.

Shaw

Kiki 3.5/5
An amalgamation of things: sports bar, patio, dance bar, one of the most popular gay bars (I always see a million people I know when I go)
Dirty Goose Closed 9/8
Closed. The first DC gay bar I went to. Known for their cute rooftop, second floor is a dance floor, first floor is cocktails.
Shakers 4/5
DC's 2nd newest gay bar, known for shaken drinks (they have this drink shaker that looks fancy but they never use it), often loud AF and too bass heavy, frequently has drag queens on the weekend
Uproar 4/5
Gay bear bar, has beer bust Sundays (unlimited food/beer for $20), one time the music was really really good but I don't go too often. There are rumors that it is closing soon.
Nellie's 3.5/5
Former epicenter of the DC gay scene, got canceled and reclaimed by the Black community, in my experience it is a Black gay bar

The best dance/circuit party I've been to in DC. The sound quality and DJ were absolutely incredible. It starts pretty tame and then gets progressively more wild throughout the evening.

at DC9 in Shaw
